Ningbo


About

Ningbo,formerly written Ningpo, is a sub-provincial city in northeast Zhejiang province in the People's Republic of China. It comprises the urban districts of Ningbo proper, three satellite cities, and a number of rural counties including islands in Hangzhou Bay and the East China Sea. Its port, spread across several locations, is among the busiest in the world and the municipality possesses a separate state-planning status. To the north, Hangzhou Bay separates Ningbo from Shanghai; to the east lies Zhoushan in the East China Sea; on the west and south, Ningbo borders Shaoxing and Taizhou respectively.

Statistics

  • Administrated area has a population of 7.6 million
  • Ningbo has a humid subtropical climate
  • The mean annual temperature is 16.53 degrees Celsius
  • The city receives an average annual rainfall of 1,440 millimetres (56.7 in)
  • From August to October, Ningbo experiences the effects of typhoons, and is affected by an average 1.8 storms annually
